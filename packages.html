Universal Standards

Standard Operating Protocol

Regardless of the package or sector, all 4 US 2 C Security deployments follow a strict, standardised operating protocol ensuring consistency, accountability, and the highest safety standards on every site.

Armed supervisor always present during operational hours
OB Books maintained for all staff on every shift
Minimum 2 officers at every shift changeover
Continuous radio contact with operations centre
Immediate incident reporting and documentation
Regular compliance and welfare checks by supervisors
Armed guard at all high-risk opening and closing periods
Zero-tolerance for dereliction of duty or post abandonment
