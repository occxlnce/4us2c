Who We Are

South African Security
Excellence

4 US 2 C Security (Pty) Ltd is a proudly South African private security company established in 2016, headquartered at Constantia Business Park, Midrand. We deliver comprehensive, individualized security solutions across South Africa and the African continent.

Founded by an industry veteran with over 8 years of experience, we take a multi-disciplinary approach — collaborating with SAPS, JMPD, Environmental Health Board, and PSIRA to deliver security that is innovative, effective, and fully compliant.
