Our Story

Who We Are

4 US 2 C Security (Pty) Ltd is a proudly South African private security company established in January 2016, headquartered at Constantia Business Park, Midrand, Johannesburg.

We deliver comprehensive, individualized security solutions across South Africa and the African continent — serving government departments, retail groups, commercial properties, estates, and individual clients.

Founded by an industry veteran with over 8 years of hands-on experience spanning safeguarding, armed response, armed guarding, and access control, we have grown into a trusted security partner known for reliability and professionalism.

We follow a multi-disciplinary approach — collaborating with SAPS, the Johannesburg Metro Police Department (JMPD), the Environmental Health Board, and PSIRA — to deliver security solutions that are innovative, effective, and fully compliant with all South African regulations.
